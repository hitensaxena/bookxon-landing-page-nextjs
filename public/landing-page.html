Got Questions?
We Have Answers.

Find answers to common questions about Crow.

How easy is it to get started with Crow? +
Getting started is simple! Sign up for a free account or paid plan, follow the quick setup guide (add services, availability), and share your unique booking link. Most users are ready to accept bookings within 10-15 minutes. We also have helpful guides and support if you need assistance.
Can I use Crow on my phone and computer? +
Absolutely! Crow is designed as a web application that works seamlessly across desktops, tablets, and mobile browsers. We also offer dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android (included with Pro and Business plans) for the best on-the-go experience.
How does Crow help reduce no-shows? +
Crow's Pro and Business plans include several features to combat no-shows: automated email/SMS reminders, the ability to require deposits or save client card details, and customizable cancellation policies with automatic fee enforcement. Users typically see a significant reduction in missed appointments.
What if I already use Google Calendar or Outlook? +
No problem! Crow offers two-way calendar sync (available on paid plans) with Google Calendar and Outlook Calendar. This prevents double bookings by blocking off time in Crow when you have external events, and vice-versa.
What kind of support do you offer? +
We offer comprehensive support! All users have access to our extensive online Help Center 24/7. Email support is available for all plans, while Pro and Business plan users benefit from priority email and chat support channels during business hours.

Still have questions? Contact our friendly support team.
